About Me

Navigating troubled waters
- together.

Hello, my name is Claire. With over 20 years of experience as a Person-Centred Therapist, EMDR Practitioner, and Clinical Supervisor, I offer a warm, safe, and non-judgemental space where you can reflect, heal, and grow - at your own pace.

I am deeply committed to empathy as the foundation of everything I do. I am also neurodivergent-affirming, LGBTQ+ inclusive, and openly respectful of spiritual and paranormal experiences. I believe in meeting you exactly where you are - whatever that looks like.

I currently work alongside a specialist suicide bereavement charity and hold a Level 7 Certificate in Clinical Supervision. My experience spans trauma, domestic violence, self-harm, eating disorders, bereavement, chronic illness, addiction, and much more.

Services

How we can work together

All sessions are held online, giving you the freedom and safety of your own space. I offer a range of therapeutic approaches tailored to your unique needs - there is no one-size-fits-all here.

🧠

Person-Centred Therapy

A warm, collaborative approach placing you at the heart of your healing. Together we explore your feelings and experiences in a space free from judgement, at the pace that feels right for you.

Level 4 Qualified · NCPS Accredited
🔵

EMDR Therapy

Eye Movement Desensitisation and Reprocessing - a powerful, evidence-based approach for processing trauma. Available for both adults and children and young people (CYP) online.

Adults & Children · £90 per session
🕊️

Suicide Bereavement

Specialist support for those who have experienced the devastating loss of a loved one to suicide. This deeply meaningful work is offered with the highest level of compassion and care.

Specialist Service · Charity Affiliated
🌱

Clinical Supervision

Level 7 Clinical Supervision for trainee and qualified counsellors. A thoughtful, supportive reflective space to develop your professional practice and wellbeing.

Level 7 Certificate · Trainee & Qualified
🧒

Children & Young People

Extensive CYP experience, formerly affiliated with CAMHS and NHS. I offer a safe, age-appropriate, neurodivergent-affirming space for young people to be heard.

CYP Therapist · CAMHS Affiliated
💜

Integrative & Eclectic

Drawing from compassion-focused therapy, narrative therapy, ecotherapy, and more - I adapt my approach to serve what you need, not a fixed model.

Humanistic · Integrative · Kink-Aware
